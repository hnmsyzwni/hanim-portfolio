"Kawan Buku Kita: Teman Bacaan Anak Kampung"

The program involving students from Years 3 to 6 at SK Bkt Tampoi (A), Dengkil was conducted with the objective of inspiring Orang Asli students by fostering their academic interests and motivation for personal development. Through engaging and interactive learning activities, the program aimed to cultivate a passion for reading and support the holistic growth of the participants.

"Community Library Outreach"

The program was held on June 22, 2024, at the Malaysian Prison Museum located on Jalan Parameswara, Kampung Bandar Hilir, Melaka. It involved 40 students from MRSM Alor Gajah, Melaka. The main goal was to share knowledge and give the students a chance to learn about and experience what it’s like inside a prison. It was a great way to expose them to a different environment and help them gain new insights.
